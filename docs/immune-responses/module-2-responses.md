## A. CORE CLAIM VULNERABILITIES

### A1. "Distinction-Space" Is Undefined Hand-Waving
**Attack:** You invoke "thermodynamic attractors in distinction-space" without defining what distinction-space IS. Is it a metric space? Topological space? Configuration space?

**Assessment:** VALID

**Proposed Response:** Define explicitly as a *metric space*:
- Points = possible states of a system
- Metric d(x,y) = minimum energy required to distinguish x from y
- Thermodynamic attractors = basins where distinction-maintenance cost is minimized

Add formal definition box grounding the metaphor in operational terms.

---

### A2. The Identity Claim Is Asserted, Not Derived
**Attack:** "Mathematical necessity IS thermodynamic stability" is a strong metaphysical identity claim. Why not mere correlation?

**Assessment:** VALID

**Proposed Response:** Two options:
1. **Weaken to supervenience:** Mathematical necessity *supervenes on* thermodynamic stability (every difference in mathematical status corresponds to a difference in stability)
2. **Argue for identity:** If two things have identical extension, identical intension, and no conceivable case separates them, identity is warranted

Lean toward (1) unless we can make strong argument for (2).

---

### A3. "Energy to Maintain 2+2=5" Is Operationally Meaningless
**Attack:** What experiment would measure this? If no conceivable measurement, this is definitional sleight-of-hand.

**Assessment:** PARTIALLY VALID

**Proposed Response:** Reframe as *limiting case thought experiment*, not empirical prediction. Operational content via proxies:
- Error-correcting codes require energy proportional to error rate
- Maintaining inconsistent databases costs more than consistent ones
- Neural patterns representing contradictions are metabolically unstable

Acknowledge this is conceptual grounding, not direct measurement.
