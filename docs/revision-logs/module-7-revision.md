## Key Corrections

### The Critical Landauer Correction

**The most important change:** Module 7 now clearly acknowledges that Landauer's principle (kT ln 2 per bit) is IMPORTED as foundational, not derived. This grounds Axiom 1.

The direction of explanation is:
- Landauer's principle (1961, confirmed 2012) --> grounds Axiom 1 --> framework builds from there
- NOT: Framework axioms --> derive Landauer's principle

### The Second Law Correction

The Second Law is INTERPRETED through distinction-vocabulary, not derived from our axioms alone. This interpretation requires importing statistical mechanics (equiprobability, typicality). The framework provides conceptual vocabulary for understanding the Second Law, not an independent derivation.

### The Complementarity Correction

Throughout the module, language now emphasizes:
- "can be understood through" instead of "follows from"
- "interpreted" instead of "derived"
- "provides vocabulary for" instead of "proves"
- The framework complements physics, not replaces it
