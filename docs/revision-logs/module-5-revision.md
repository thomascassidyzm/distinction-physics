### What We Genuinely DERIVE (from axioms):
- Effective discreteness: No continuous quantity can be accessed by any OLU
- Resolution hierarchy: Different OLUs access different grain sizes based on energy budgets

- Superposition as undistinguished possibilities
- Uncertainty as resource allocation
- Measurement as distinction-making
- Entanglement as shared distinction structure
- Classical emergence through resolution economics
- Wave-particle duality
- Quantum interpretations (Copenhagen, Many-Worlds, etc.)

### What We IMPORT (foundational, not derived):
- The Born rule ($|alpha|^2$ probabilities)
- Hilbert space structure and complex amplitudes
- The Schrodinger equation and unitary evolution
- The measurement postulate and projection operators
- Planck's constant and its specific value
- Landauer's limit ($kT ln 2$ per bit)
- Decoherence theory
- Bell inequality violations and EPR correlations
- Quantum computing formalism

### What We CONJECTURE (speculative extensions):
- Specific measurement energy scaling laws
- Coherence-complexity scaling relationships
- Modified uncertainty relations near critical resolution scales

## Key Themes of the Revision

### 1. Interpretation, Not Derivation
The module now consistently frames QM interpretation as providing conceptual vocabulary, not deriving quantum mechanics from first principles. QM remains as established physics.

### 2. Complementarity, Not Replacement
The framework is positioned as complementary to physics. We don't replace the Schrodinger equation, Born rule, or Hilbert space---we interpret them through distinction vocabulary.

### 3. Epistemic Labels Throughout
Every major claim now carries one of four labels:
- **[DERIVED]**: Genuinely follows from axioms (effective discreteness, resolution hierarchy)
- **[INTERPRETED]**: Existing physics reframed through distinction vocabulary (most claims)
- **[IMPORTED]**: Brought in from established physics (Born rule, Planck's constant, etc.)
- **[CONJECTURED]**: Speculative extensions (modified uncertainty relations, scaling laws)

### 4. Humble Claims About Interpretation Debates
Section 5.10 no longer claims to "resolve" or "explain" QM interpretations. It offers complementary vocabulary for understanding them.

### 5. Honest About Imports
Explicit acknowledgment of what we import:
- Born rule and complex amplitudes
- Hilbert space structure
- Schrodinger equation
- Planck's constant
- Landauer's limit
- Decoherence theory
