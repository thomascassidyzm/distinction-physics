# Changelog: V7 → V8

*Distinction as Primitive: Major Revision*
*December 2025*

---

## Overview

V8 represents a comprehensive revision of the treatise following 7 rounds of adversarial testing with 36 targeted attacks. The primary achievement is establishing **epistemic honesty throughout**: the framework is now consistently positioned as a philosophical meta-theory that interprets existing physics, not a replacement for it.

---

## Major Changes

### 1. Framework Positioning

**Before (V7):** Framed as deriving physics from pure ontology
**After (V8):** Positioned as "philosophical meta-theory complementary to physics"

- Abstract rewritten to acknowledge interpretive (not derivational) nature
- Title emphasis shifted from "Distinction Physics" to "Distinction as Primitive"
- Claims about "generating" or "deriving" physics replaced with "interpreting" and "consistent with"

### 2. Epistemic Labeling System

Introduced consistent labeling throughout all modules:

| Label | Meaning | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| `[DERIVED]` | Follows logically from axioms | Effective discreteness theorem |
| `[INTERPRETED]` | Existing physics reframed through distinction-vocabulary | QM measurement as distinction-creation |
| `[IMPORTED]` | Brought in from established physics | Landauer's principle, Born rule, Einstein field equations |
| `[CONJECTURED]` | Speculative extension beyond what axioms force | Spacetime as distinction-pattern |
| `[POST-HOC]` | Retroactive consistency, not prediction | Most "predictions" in Module 8 |

### 3. Landauer Relationship Clarified

**Before (V7):** Ambiguous whether Landauer was derived or assumed
**After (V8):** Explicitly marked as IMPORTED

- Axiom 1 now clearly states it imports Landauer's principle
- The framework interprets Landauer thermodynamically; it does not derive it
- Removes false impression of deriving thermodynamics from pure ontology

### 4. Hard Problem Repositioned

**Before (V7):** Suggested framework "dissolves" or "transforms" the hard problem
**After (V8):** Hard problem is "RELOCATED, not solved"

- Module 3 revised throughout with editorial notes
- Glossary entry updated
- Honest acknowledgment: we provide vocabulary, not explanation
- "Why distinction-making feels like something" remains unexplained

### 5. Key Contribution Domain Identified

**Before (V7):** All domains treated with similar weight
**After (V8):** Module 4 (Learning) identified as KEY CONTRIBUTION DOMAIN

- Learning as anti-entropic distinction building is the framework's strongest claim
- Practical validation through implemented systems (SSi, Zenjin, Alexander)
- Other domains offer interpretation; learning offers actionable theory

### 6. Module 6 (Spacetime) Overhauled

**Before (V7):** Circularity between OLUs and spacetime noted but unaddressed
**After (V8):** Transcendental framing resolves apparent circularity

New additions:
- Three defensible interpretations (transcendental, mutual constitution, dispositional realism)
- Operationalization of "distinction" with physical criteria
- Partial quantification: E(d) ≥ kT ln 2 · f(d/ℓ_P)
- Specific falsifiable prediction (Conjecture 6.1: gravity-coherence coupling)
- Engagement with philosophy of spacetime literature
- Explicit connection to Module 5 (parallel constraint structure)

### 7. Module 8 (Predictions) Reframed

**Before (V7):** Presented as "predictions"
**After (V8):** Reframed as "consistency demonstrations"

- Title changed to "Empirical Consistency and Testability"
- Acknowledged post-hoc nature of most "predictions"
- Distinguished framework-specific predictions from generic ones
- Added falsifiability table with clear criteria

### 8. Module 9 (Synthesis) Revised

**Before (V7):** "Bold Claim" language
**After (V8):** "Honest Claim" / "Honest Assessment"

- Problems described as "reframed" not "dissolved"
- Limitations explicitly catalogued
- Success criteria honestly evaluated
