- Transcendental priority of distinction (you cannot state any theory without first making distinctions)
- Effective discreteness
- Resolution limits
- Finitude
- Some aspects of learning as distinction pattern building

### What We INTERPRET (existing physics through distinction-vocabulary):
- Quantum mechanics (superposition, measurement)
- Thermodynamics (entropy as distinction-decay)
- Consciousness (self-referential distinction-making)
- Mathematics (formalization of distinction-patterns)
- Most of learning framework

### What We IMPORT (foundational, not derived):
- Landauer's principle (kT ln 2 per bit)
- Fundamental constants (h, c, G, k)
- Statistical mechanics framework
- Quantum formalism (Born rule, Hilbert space)

### What We CONJECTURE (speculative extension):
- Spacetime as distinction-geometry
- Quantum gravity within the framework
- Observer-dependent physics
- Quantum effects in cognition
