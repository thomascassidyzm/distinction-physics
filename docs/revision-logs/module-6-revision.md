## Attacks Addressed

### Priority 1: ATK-6.03 (OLU Presupposes What It Explains) and ATK-6.27 (Who Draws First Distinction?)

**Attack:** OLUs presuppose spacetime; claiming spacetime emerges from OLUs is circular.

**Response:** We now adopt an explicit **transcendental interpretation** (Section 6.0):
- We describe necessary structure for physics-as-accessible-to-observers, not causal origins
- Three defensible interpretations offered:
  1. Transcendental (Kantian): Conditions for possible experience, not causal claims
  2. Mutual Constitution: OLUs and spacetime co-arise, neither prior
  3. Dispositional Realism: Structure exists for-possible-observers even before actual observers
- The circularity is addressed by clarifying we are not making ontogenetic claims

1. Infinite-precision spacetime access is impossible for finite-energy observers
2. A minimum distinguishable scale must exist
3. Different observers access different effective resolutions (resolution hierarchy)

### What Module 6 INTERPRETS (consistent with but not forced by axioms):
1. Spacetime as distinction-pattern vocabulary
2. The minimum scale corresponds to Planck scale (requires imported physics)
3. Relational structure reflects distinction-making (transcendental framing)

2. Special relativity: Lorentz group SO(3,1), invariant speed c
