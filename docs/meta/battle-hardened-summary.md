- Quantum uncertainty (resolution requires energy)
- Thermodynamic irreversibility (maintaining order costs energy)
- Cognitive limitations (processing requires metabolism)

### The Framework Identity

**Distinction Physics is a transcendental meta-theory.**

This means:
- It describes **conditions of possible physics** for any OLU
- It does NOT derive physics causally (OLUs don't cause physics)
- Physics and OLUs are **mutually constituting** aspects of one structure
- Compare to Kant: conditions of experience, not its cause

**Ontic Structural Realism with Energetic Grounding**

- What exists is structure (relational, not substantial)
- Observers are part of the structure, not outside it
- Observer-dependence ≠ mind-dependence (not idealism)
- Structure requires energy to maintain (thermodynamic grounding)
